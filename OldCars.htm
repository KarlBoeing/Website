ADAC-Strassen-Hilfsdienst (roadside assistance service)
was established in 1928.

ADAC was originally named Deutsche Motorradfahrer-Vereinigung (German Motorcyclist Association) and re-named Allgemeiner Deutscher Automobil-Club (ADAC) in 1911

These motorcycle cabs from the Deutsche Kraftwagenwerke Berlin-Spandau were quite popular.


<

The passengers were happy to accept the cramped transport, as the tariff was only half that of a public Taxi.

From 1922 to 1932 around 60,000 units were built before the cars also adjusted their tariffs downwards.
