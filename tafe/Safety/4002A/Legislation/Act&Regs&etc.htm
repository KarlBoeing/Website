Access to specific sections

of OS&H Act and Regulations

It is essential that you become familiar with the Occupational Safety and Health Act. Unfortunately links to specific sections of the Act cannot be made. There are some links in a contents page but you should download the Act as a pdf-doc from the State Law Publisher site. In this case you will always have a current edition of the Act.


If you have downloaded the Act then click on the bookmark icon as shown in the opposite figure.

(you can also download the Act as a pdf-file, MS-word or a html-file).

However, the most appropriate download is using a pdf-file.
If you click on the bookmark icon then it will expand and you'll see the contents of all parts, divisions, and section as shown in the opposite figure. If you click on on any section, then that specific section of the Act will be shown.













Please use the slider bar to scroll up or down.

There is no need to buy the Act or the Regulations because it's easy to download it from the Internet and then access the necessary information.

The same principle as outlined above applies for the Occupational Safety and Health Regulations.

There are quizzes that will test your knowledge of the Act and Regulations.

For the quizzes you need the OS&H Act and OS&H Regulations open on your screen if you don't have a hard copy at hand.

However, before you access a quiz make sure the Act and Regulation are open on your screen.

If you open a quiz you must finish the quiz otherwise you'll not be able to open or reopen the quiz. Quizzes must be completed in a given time and if you don't submit the quiz the time is still running. That's the reason while a quiz can't be open because it's still waiting for input. So make sure quizzes are finished and submitted as it should be.

Occupational Safety and Health Regulations

Some knowledge of the Occupational Safety and Health Act and Regulations is required to pass the unit 'manage occupational health and safety in the building and construction workplace' also no specific reference is made to the Act or Regulations. How could one make a risk assessment if one has no knowledge of the contents of the Act and Regulations?

Download the Occupational Safety and Health Regulations and find the relevant parts that you should know. The most important parts for this unit are Part 3 - Workplace safety, Part 4 - Plant and Part 5 - Hazardous substances. The regulations that you must know by hart is 3.1 and 3.2 .
