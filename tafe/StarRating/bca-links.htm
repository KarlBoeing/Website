BCA Energy Requirements

The Building Code of Australia is produced and maintained by the Australian Building Codes Board (ABCB) on behalf of the Commonwealth, State and Territory Governments. The BCA has been given the status of building regulations by all States and Territories.

The ABCB keeps you up-to-date with Energy Efficiency Measures for residential and commercial buildings.
