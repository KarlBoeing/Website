What is Intumescent paint ?

  • Intumescent paint, intumescent coatings, or fire retardant paint all have the same goal, to stop fire damaging and heating the surface underneath the paint. Temperatures found in a fire situation cause the intumescent paint to swell and produce an insulating barrier that restricts the rate of increase in temperature of the substance underneath.
