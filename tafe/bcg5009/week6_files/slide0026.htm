Common fire protection materials used for structural steel include sprayed fire-resistive materials (SFRM), thin-film intumescents, epoxybased intumescents/mastics.
Sprayed Fire-Resistive Materials (SFRM) SFRM fall into two broad categories: sprayed mineral fi ber SFRM (dry-mix) and sprayed cementitious SFRM (wet-mix). All SFRM are composed of varying mixtures of mineral fi bers, Portland cement, binders, and water.
