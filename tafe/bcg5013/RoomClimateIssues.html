Room Climate Components

The comfortableness is an important factor for the well being and good health of Persons. How comfortable it is depends to a great extent on the quality of the room climate. The room climate is of importance because we stay on average two-third of our life in living rooms and offices. Air temperature, surface temperature of floor, ceiling and walls, humidity an air movement are majors factors for the comfortableness of human beings as shown in Figure 1

When measuring the thermal indoor climate, it's important to remember that you don't feel the room temperature, you feel the heast loss from your body. The parameters theat must be measured are those which affect the energy loss of your body.

The above factors determine the heat transfer from our body to the surounding environment, which is mainly related to the room air temperature as shown in Figure 2 and Figure 3.

The main variables are:

  • Temperature (air, radiant, surface)
  • Relative Humidity
  • Air Velosity
  • Activity
  • Clothing
