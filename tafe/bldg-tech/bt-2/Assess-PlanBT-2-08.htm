NOTE:
Candidates will need to demonstrate competence a number of times under various conditions to satisfy the Element/Performance Criteria
Under CBT and AQTF provisions candidates do not receive a percentage mark for the unit completed.
Candidates demonstrating competence will receive a grade of “CO” (competent). If a pass of the four (4) elements of Assessment is not achieved at first attempt, an 'H' grade is to be applied. The student can resubmit Assessment for second attempt to clear the 'H' grade. The date for the second sumission is 7 days after due date. If the student does not PASS the second attempt within the specified time an 'R' grade will be applied.
Candidates failing to demonstrate competence at the end of the unit will receive the grade of “R” (re-enrol), and will be required to re-enrol in the unit again and complete ALL assessment tasks again to the required competence level.
All students must attend the presentaions in Week 16 & 17. Students who miss a presentation will receive a R-grade.
