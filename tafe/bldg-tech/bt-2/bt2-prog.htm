Module purposes

This module develops the learner’s ability to resolve construction problems for commercial buildings up to an effective height of 25m and industrial buildings.

Learning outcome 1   Resolve construction problems in relation to construction techniques/methods, use and application of materials.

Learning outcome 2   Resolve construction problems using alternative construction methods.

Learning outcome 3   Solve on-site problems which can commonly occur with building materials used in commercial and industrial buildings.

 

The assignment should preferably cover the following issues:

Evaluate and resolve construction problems with regard to:

·            Refurbishing

·            Restoration

·            Renovation

Sketch/detail alternative construction details/methods to resolve construction problems.

Prepare and evaluate construction details to compare the effects on the construction process.

Solve on-site problems with building materials.
