Building Technology 2

Assignment Topics

Students need to investigate aspects of design and construction having, primarily, to do with the refurbishing, restoration, and renovation of older Perth buildings. Students can select a topic from the list below or alternatively select a specific topic that is of special interest to you but isn't listed. However, your own selected topic needs the approval of the lecturer.

It may not be easy to get sufficient information for your assignment topic. It will take a lot of phone calls and running around to find out whether you get adequate information to complete your topic. Keep in mind that contacts and/or documents, required for your assignment, from architects, contractors, builders, owners or/and construction managers often are hard to get.

Before you submit your synopsis make sure you selected a topic for which you can gather adequate information to complete the task. Choosing a different topic at a later stage will create a severe setback and you may not be able to finish the module. Please take time to do your topic-research thoroughly.

    1. Ecological Building

    Research and document the materials and methods associated with Ecological Building including Environmental considerations - Material source, Transport, reduce quantity of material, re-used building materials from demolished buildings particularly bricks, stone, steel beams, and timber joists, recycling and disposal of construction or demolition waste.

    Consider BCA requirements and Australian standards

    1. Two storey construction in cyclonic regions of WA

    Research and document the materials and methods associated with construction of two storey buildings in cyclonic regions (roof, wall footing), structural connections, fenestration, bracing against racking forces etc.

    Consider BCA requirements and Australian standards

     

    1. Transportable / relocatable homes/buildings

    Research and document the materials and methods associated with prefabricated construction of Transportable homes/buildings including transportation, erection wall frames, roof framing, exterior cladding, interior linings, windows, insulation, interior linings, doors, appliances, plumbing, etc (no container houses or inflatable structures)

    Consider BCA requirements and Australian standards
