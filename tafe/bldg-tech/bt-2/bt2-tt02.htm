    Attendance for all shaded sessions is compulsory.
The success of the program depends very much on your commitment to participate as well as the attendance of all compulsory sessions. Students who do not submit their assessments on due day have an opportunity to re-submit assessments the following week. If they fail to do so receive an R-grade and need to re-enrole.
If a pass of the four (4) elements of Assessment is not achieved at first attempt, an 'H' grade is to be applied. The student can resubmit/resit Assessments for second attempt to clear the 'H' grade. The date for the second sumission/test is the following week. If the student does not PASS the second attempt within the specified time an 'R' grade is to be applied.
