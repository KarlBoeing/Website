Building Surveying Course


Certificate IV Building & Construction (Building)
CPC40108

This qualification will provide you with the practical skills and knowledge to work as a builder and manager of a small-medium building business dealing with low rise construction projects. You will practice a diverse range of skills that will enable you to plan and supervise construction work and prepare, cost and schedule for a contract. You will learn about building codes and standards, legal requirements and structural principles. You will apply these skills to low rise residential and commercial constructions.

Diploma of Building & Construction (Building)
CPC50210- 741

This qualification will provide you with the practical skills and knowledge to work as a builder on medium rise building projects. You will practice a diverse range of skills that will enable you to supervise the planning of construction work, ensure the quality of the work and manage the risks associated with projects. You will learn about building codes and standards and structural principles related to medium rise buildings.

The Diploma of Building is an advanced course that trains you in Building Codes, Standards and Principles, tendering, planning, estimating, costing and scheduling of residential, industrial and commercial buildings.

Diploma of Building Surveying

BCG50108 - WA 614
This qualification will provide you with the practical skills and knowledge to assess the construction of domestic scale buildings, produce working drawings for residential buildings, undertake site surveys and set out procedures for building projects, apply building codes and standards to residential buildings, apply principles of energy efficient design to buildings, apply building control legislation to building surveying and interact with clients in a regulated environment.
