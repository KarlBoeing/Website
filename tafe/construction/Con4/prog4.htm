Semester Program

Because of the duplications in the Construction syllabus documents the main focus in Construction 4 will be on:
Prepare construction details of commercial and residential buildings to an effective height of 25 metres.

The unit is project based. A 7 (seven) storey office building (click Project) will be used not only to cover the syllabus content but more importantly to join all topics and issues regarding the construction of an entire building.
Students need to research allocated topics, which will then be discussed in class. A written report and construction drawings must be submitted on the specified date and students need to present their findings orally and try to generate a discussion of the researched topic in class.
