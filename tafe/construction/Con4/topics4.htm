Project topics (commercial office building)

We will investigate and discussed all issues associated with this project in greater detail in lectures . The lecturer will give students an allocated topic from the list below. Students need to research and prepare a written report including construction details (part assessment) drawn to an appropriate scale and give an oral presentation of their work to the class.

Discussion and presentation topics

1. Foundation, bearing capacity of soils, footing types.
2. De-watering, water proofing & tanking.
3. Underpinning, retaining walls, ground anchors.
4. Building construction method and stability issues
        Concrete   – steel – composite
        Structural materials
5. Structural floor systems
        In situ concrete
        Pre-cast, pre-stressed concrete
       Composite structure
6. Structural walls and columns
         Steel
         Fire resistance considerations
7. BCA requirements for fire and sound transmission
8. Front and rear façade
        Claddings
        Windows
        Curtin walls
9. Suspended ceilings and partitions
        Fire compartments
        Wall & ceiling system (material selection)
        Fire & sound requirements
10. Air conditioning and ducting, drainage and plumbing requirement

Assessment

Written report 25%
Construction drawings 25%
Oral presentation 10%
Participation 10%
Semester examination 30%

A pass rate for each assessment component of 50% is required to pass the unit. More information about assessment is given in the assessment structure sheet.
