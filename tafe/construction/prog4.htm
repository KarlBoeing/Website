Construction 4

 

Semester    Program

 

Because of the duplications in the Construction syllabi documents the main focus in Construction 4 will be on:

                           Prepare construction details of commercial and residential buildings to an effective height of 25 metres.

 

The unit is project based. A 6 (six) storey office building (refer to Figure 1) will be used not only to cover the syllabus content but more importantly to join all topics and issues regarding the construction of an entire building.

Students need to research allocated topics, which will then be discussed in class. A written report and construction drawings must be submitted on the specified date and students need to present their findings orally and try to generate a discussion of the researched topic in class.

The attached key competencies and ability statement (at AQF III) are an integral part of this unit.

Assessment

To complete the subject satisfactorily the following five assessment components are required:
Written report                 25%
Construction drawings     25%
Oral presentations           10%
Participation                    10%
Examination                     30% (Week18)
A pass rate for each assessment component of 50% is required to pass the unit. More details are found on the assessment structure sheet.

The attendance for the compulsory session is very important for the successful completion of this subject. If you cant commit yourself to the above timetable you better withdraw from the subject.

An attendance penalty of 4 marks for each missing compulsory session applies.
