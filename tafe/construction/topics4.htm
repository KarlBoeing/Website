Construction 4

Project topics (commercial office building)

 

Based on the project the topics below will be investigated and discussed in greater detail. Students need to research allocated topics and prepare a written report including construction details drawn to an appropriate scale and give an oral presentation of their work to the class.

 

Discussion and presentation topics

 

1.   Foundation, bearing capacity of soils, footing types.

2.   De-watering, water proofing & tanking.

3.      Underpinning, retaining walls, ground anchors.

4.   Building construction method and stability issues.

·       Concrete – steel – composite

·       Structural materials

5.   Structural floor systems

·       In situ concrete

·       Pre-cast, pre-stressed concrete

·       Composite structure

6.   Structural walls and columns

·        Concrete

·        Steel

·        Fire resistance considerations

7.   BCA requirements for fire and sound transmission

8.   Front and rear façade

·        Claddings

·        Windows

·        Curtin walls

9.   Suspended ceilings and partitions

·        Fire compartments

·        Wall & ceiling system (material selection)

·        Fire & sound requirements

10. Air conditioning and ducting, drainage and plumbing requirements

 

Assessment

Written report                            25%

Construction drawings                25%

Oral presentation                       10%

Participation                              10%

End of semester examination      30%

A pass rate for each assessment component of 50% is required to pass the unit. More information about assessment is given in the assessment structure sheet. An attendance penalty of 4 marks for each missing compulsory session applies.
