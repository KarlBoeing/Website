COURSE OUTLINE

Module Title:             BUILDERS’ WORKING DRAWING 2

 

Nominal Duration:      60 Hours

 

Module Code:            ABC062

 

W.A. SIN:                   27 743

 

Module Purpose:        Further develop the students skills in plan and specification reading and interpretation, and drafting and sketching skills forlow rise residential buildings.

 

Co-Requisite:             Construction 2

                                    Building Technology 1

                                    Materials 1

 

Text Required:           Architectural Drawing Standards and Conventions

                                    Basic Training Manual 14.10, PLAN READING.

 

Reference Texts:       Appropriate Australian Standards; Glossary of Building and Planning Terms; AS 1100 Architectural Drawing and Supplement 1.

 

Course Content

Synopsis:                      Plan Reading; Upper Storey Extension; Building Details 1,
  Building Details 2, Building Details 3.


The Project:               The task is to build an upper storey extension for a single storey residential house as shown in Figure 1 below. You need to redraw the existing house and and the upper storey floor plan, section and sufficient details. The extent of the working drawings will be discussed in class.


Figure 1

 

Assessment

Details:                       This module has five (5) assessment elements; two (2) written plan reading Assignment PLUS three (3) drawing Assignments.

 

                                    A pass in each of the five (5) elements of Assessment is required to pass the Module.  Under CBT and AQTF provisions candidates do not receive a percentage mark for the module completed. Candidates demonstrating competence will receive a grade of “CO” (competent).

                                    If a pass is not achieved in any of Assignments at first attempt, an ‘H’ grade is to be applied.  The student must resubmit Assignment(s) that receive a "H" grade and PASS within the enrolment period or an ‘R’ grade is to be applied.

 

Assessment

Methods:                    Plan Reading (Assessment 1 & 5) to be from supplied plans. The assessment comprises selected questions from the plans and a drawing detail.

 

                                    Drawing Assignments (Assignments 1, 2 & 3) to be completed in ink using drafting pens on tracing paper or film and submitted with a print copy to the lecturer by the nominated date given for each Assignment.
