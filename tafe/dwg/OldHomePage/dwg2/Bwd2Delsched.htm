NOTE:

Candidates will need to demonstrate competence a number of times under various conditions to satisfy the Learning outcomes/Assessment Criteria.

Under CBT and AQTF provisions candidates do not receive a percentage mark for the module completed.

Candidates demonstrating competence will receive a grade of “CO” (competent).

Candidates failing to demonstrate competence at the end of an individual assessment task will receive the grade of “H” (Hold), and will be required to resubmit the drawing(s)

Candidates failing to demonstrate competence at the end of the module will receive the grade of “R” (re-enrol), and will be required to re-enrol in the module again and complete ALL assessment tasks again to the required competence level.

Candidates failing and will be required to re-enrol in the module again and complete ALL assessment tasks again to the required competence level.
