NOTE:
Candidates will need to demonstrate competence a number of times under various conditions to satisfy the Learning outcomes/Assessment Criteria. Under CBT and AQTF provisions candidates do not receive a percentage mark for the module completed. Candidates demonstrating competence will receive a grade of “CO” (competent).

Candidates failing to demonstrate competence at the end of an individual assessment task will receive the grade of “H” (Hold), and will be required to resubmit the drawing(s) If a pass is not achieved in any of the Assessments at first attempt, an 'H' grade is to be applied. Candidates must submit or sit the tests for second attempt to clear the 'H' grade. A 'H' grade for any Assessment must be cleared within one Week after the date of the test or your submission. .

If the student does not PASS the second attempt within the stipulated Resubmission/re-sit time frame an 'R' grade is to be applied. Candidates failing to demonstrate competence at the end of the module will also receive the grade of “R” grade, and will be required to re-enrol in the module again and complete ALL assessment tasks again to the required competence level.
