Module Title:

 BUILDERS' WORKING DRAWING 2
Nominal Duration: 60 Hours
Module Code: ABC062 WA. SIN: 27743
Module purposes: To develop the students skills in plan and specification reading and interpretation, and drafting and sketching skills for low rise residential buildings
Pre-requisites: 27661     ABC061   Builders' Working Drawing 1
Co-requisites 27671     ABC001    Construction 1
Text Required: Kevin Holtom "Architectural Drawing"
Reference Texts: Appropriate Australian Standards; Glossary of Building and Planning Terms; AS 1100 Architectural Drawing and Supplement 1.
Course Content
Synopsis		 Plan/specification interpretation; Working Drawings (scale 1:100); Sections & Building Details to specified scales (orthographic & pictorial drawings)
Delivery Module: The Module activities will take place on Tuesday of each week (dates see Program) in Room 1312 and commences at 13:00. Duration of each activity is three (3) hours in Semester 1 - 2008.
General drawing requirements set of drawing for council approval, working drawings and details). The main emphasis is place on the construction a second storey addition. Refer to project details for more information. Construction issues as well as the appropriate details will be discussed in class. It is important that students attend each session. Students who are not attend each class will get no additional assistance
Assessment Settings:
 This module has five (5) assessment elements.
Submission dates are outlined below:
1. Drawing Assignment 1   Submission of three (3) sheets in Week 7
     (Re-submission or late submission due in week 8)

2. Drawing Assignment 2   Submission of two (2) sheets in Week 13
     (Re-submission or late submission due in week 8)

3. Assessment                   Plan reading/drawing assessment (Week 16)
     (Resit in Week 17)

4. Drawing assignment 3   Submission of two (2) sheets in Week 17
     (Re-submission or late submission due in week 18)

5. Continuous Assessment Week1 to Week 17 (Student questioning
and observation during class times.)

A pass in each of the four (5) elements of Assessment is required to pass the Subject.
If a pass is not achieved in any of Assignments (1, 2, & 3) or Assessment (1) at first attempt, an H-grade is to be applied. The student must submit or sit a test for second attempt to clear the H-grade. A H-grade for Assignment 1, 2 & 3 or plan reading assessment must be cleared within one Week after the due date of that particular submission. Class attendance is mandatory to pass Assesment 5. If the student does not PASS the second attempt within the stipulated resubmission/re-sit time frame an 'R' grade is to be applied.

Please note in your diary the due date for all submission!
Assessment Methods: Assignment 1 Floor plans (ground & upper floor), all four (2) elevations scale 1;100; section 1-1 scale 1:50.
Assignment 2 Specified detail drawings two (2) sheets. (A details may consist of two or more views.)
Assessment 1  Plan reading from existing plans + specified detail drawing )
Assignment 3 Specified detail drawings two (2) sheets. (A detail may consist of two or more views.)
Assessment 2 (continuous) Students competency and will be assessed by questioning and observation during class times. Class attendance is mandatory.
A checklist will be used to assess students.
