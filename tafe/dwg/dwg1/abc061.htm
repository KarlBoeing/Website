MODULE TITLE                  Builders' Working Drawings 1

 

Nominal duration                   35 - 40 hours

 

Module codes

            DETAFE(SA)             CDAF

            National                      ABC061

 

Discipline code                       1001110

 

Module purpose                     To provide the participants with basic skills required to read and interpret plans and specifications and undertake basic architectural drafting.

 

Relationship to competency standards

                                                No Competency Standards are currently available.

 

Pre-requisites                         None

 

Summary of content               Size of sheets, Paper Type

                                                Use of drawing equipment

                                                Use of pencil and ink pens

                                                Lettering and numbering

                                                Setting out of drawing

                                                Use of correct scales on drawings

                                                Symbols used

                                                Dimensioning simple buildings

                                                Drawing large scale sections

                                                Interrelationships between working drawings and specifications

                                                Regulation and codes relating to drawing practice.

 

Delivery of the module          This module can be delivered in any way which allows students to acquire the skills and knowledge necessary to achieve the learning outcomes.

 

Assessment method               Short answer tests, written reports, drawing projects, drawing folio and oral questioning.

 

Conditions of assessment      It is assumed that most of the training related to this module will be off the job.

                                                Learners will be required to demonstrate safe work practices.

                                                Learners will be provided with a list of possible sources of information and references.

                                                During assessment students shall have access to permitted references.

 

Learning outcomes                On completion of this module the learner will be able to:

 

Learning outcome 1               Use drawing instruments, equipment and materials.

 

Assessment criteria               1.1 Demonstrate to a satisfactory standard the use of drawing instruments, equipment and materials by line work, simple geometric shapes, lettering, numbering, correct setting out of drawings and the use of scale.

                                                1.2 All drawings to be in accordance with current 

                                                Australian Standards.

 

Learning outcome 2               Produce drawings at varying scales using architectural conventions for line work, lettering and symbols.

 

Assessment criteria               2.1 Execute line work in a range of different types and media.

                                                2.2 Hand letter text in a variety of formats.

                                                2.3 Use different drawing scales.

                                                2.4 Show a knowledge of accepted graphic symbols.

                                                2.5 Use orthographic projection in building drafting applications.

                                                2.6 Add notation and dimensions.

 

Learning outcome 3               Read and interpret plans and specifications for a single storey dwelling.

 

Assessment criteria               3.1 Explain the interrelationship between plans and specifications.

                                                3.2 Identify, locate and interpret key information.

 

Suggested learning resources

                                                State Building Regulations Incorporating Building Code of Australia.

                                                AS3700 Masonry in Buildings

                                                AS1100 Architectural Drawing and Supplement

                                                AS2870 Residential Slabs and Footings

                                                AS1684 National Timber Framing Code and Supplement

                                                Other relevant Codes and Technical information.
