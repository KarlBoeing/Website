Two stages in building fires
•
•The two stages to be considered in the fire safety design of buildings in relation to building materials and structures are the
•
–Initial stage and the
–Fully stage.
•
•In the initial fire, the building contents such as furniture etc, are of major importance both for the initiation of the fire and its development.
•
•Surface linings may also play an important role in the initial fire, especially in escape routes and limitations of their reaction to fire is required in most building codes.
•
•In the fully developed fire, the performance of load bearing and separating structures is important in order to limit the fire to the room or fire compartment of origin. This is called the fire resistance of the building structure.
