What do we know about the fire performance
of plastics in fire?
Reaction to fire
Testing in relation to reaction to fire, deals specifically with parameters such as:
► Ease of ignitability,
► Flammability,
► Spread of flame characteristics,
► Heat evolved
► Smoke
► Toxicity
Properties relate to the contribution of a plastic material to the smoke of a fire that has already started.
Testing in this area is applicable for wall and ceiling lining materials.
