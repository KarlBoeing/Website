Smoke Production and Handling
The rate of air entrainment into the column of hot gases and unburnt solid particles above the seat of a fire will depend on:
· the perimeter of the fire
· the heat output of the fire
· the effective height of the column of hot gases above the fire.
This is, the distance between the floor of the room or space where the fire is and the bottom of the layer of smoke and hot gases which form under the ceiling of an enclosed space.
