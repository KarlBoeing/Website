Visibility
Visibility in smoke depends on many conditions:
· the colour of the smoke;
· the size of the smoke particles;
· the density of the smoke; and
· the irritant nature of the smoke are all characteristics   which influence the degree of visibility.
The physical and mental state of the observer in panic or near-panic state of a real fire will affect visibility.
