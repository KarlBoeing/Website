Systems of smoke control
There are various methods and design arrangements for smoke control in buildings. These could be one of the following:
• take advantage of the natural forces of air movement to achieve the desired control and subsequent removal of the smoke
• rely on mechanical equipment and installations to guide the air and smoke along predetermined paths leading to a point of discharge outside the building.
Either system should be considered as a means of smoke control. The most efficient smoke control is generally obtained by employing both basic systems, natural and mechanical.
