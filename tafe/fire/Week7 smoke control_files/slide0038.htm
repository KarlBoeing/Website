Smoke modelling computer packages
A number of commercial software packages are available in Australia. They use accepted models which can help in the prediction of fire growth and movement, they are:
• Firecalc – developed by the CSIRO
• Yardstick – developed by the University of Adelaide.
These are specialist programs for use by fire engineers. It should be remembered that they only give indications and cannot be regarded as being a true reflection of fire outcomes in every instance.
