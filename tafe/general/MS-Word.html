Microsoft Word Content Page

If you use a MS-Word processing application to write your assignment then a content page can easily be inserted into your document.

When you insert the Table of Contents make sure the cursor is placed at the the beginning of the document.

Word uses the headings (subheadings) (see below) in your document to build an automatic table of contents.

that can be updated when you change the heading text, sequence, or level.

How to do it is shown below

  a) On the toolbar ribbon, select  > References.

  b) Near the left end, select  > Insert Table of Contents.

Word uses the headings in your document to build an automatic table of contents that can be updated when you change the heading text, sequence, or level.

The table of contents is inserted, showing the headings and subheadings and also the page numbering in your document.
