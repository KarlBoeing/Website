How Trees Grow
•In humans and other animals growth can occur in most parts of the body. Trees do not grow like this.
•A tree grows in two ways. The tips of its branches and tips of its roots contain cells that reproduce, making the tree taller and its roots deeper.  Trunk grows by division of cambial cells immediately beneath the bark & crown size increases (enlargement of branches & production of new ones.
