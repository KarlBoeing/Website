Area selection

TASK:

Future development at eastern site of the Leederville Campus requires an “as built” plan (site plan with the exact location of all buildings including RL’s as shown below.

The eastern college site (see opposite sketch) is to be surveyed.

The site is spilt-up in three (3) areas that a group of students need to survey.

A group of three maximum four students may be formed depending on the scope of work

It is important part of this exercise is teamwork. Students must be able to work together to complete the given task.

Group A


responsible for the northern section as shown in the opposite sketch.

Students in this group must work closely with group B


Group B

responsible for the middle section as shown in the opposite sketch.

Students in this group must work closely with group A and C

Group C


responsible for the middle section as shown in the opposite sketch.

Students in this group must work closely with group B



Refer to Instructions to Students for general subject requirements. The rise & fall method must be used for leveling. All RL's can be established with a simple surveying level. For all height measurements a theodolite must be used. How to operate a theodolite will be explained in Week 7 & 8.

As most of the areas are paved, concreted or bitumised contour lines are not required. However, sufficient spot level must be shown at all wall faces of buildings and conreted or bitumised areas (if needed at distances of approx. 10 metres). Show also finished floor levels (FFL), underground service features, retaining walls or other structures.

It is the responsibility of the Groups to prepare the plan for their destignated area at a scale 1:200. All groups must work together to finish a site plan in a scale 1:500 (Overlapping and reference aligning marks are important to combine all single drawings (scale 1:200) into one drawing scale 1:500.)

Refer to Submission requirements regarding the outlay of your drawing. Students may use a CAD program to produce the required drawings. All your field measurement sketches with the necessary measurements for buildings, distances between them and to boundaries must be attached to your final submission.

If you are not sure about the scope of work discuss the matter with me.
