Drawing submission requirements

All your single submissions will be compiled into one drawing of a scale 1:200. Therefore all groups must ensure that their drawing matches the drawing of the neighboring groups. Consistency in line work and lettering is of outmost importance. It is essential that certain basic information be shown on every drawing like perimeter of buildings, stairs, kerbs, pavings, garden walls, stormwater grates, underground services, etc.

Common pen size for line work:
College boundaries0.70
Building outlines0.50
Retaining & garden walls0.35
Contour lines0.25

Common lettering thickness and sizes:
HeightThickness
Spot levels2.0 mm0.25
Finish Floor levels7.0 mm0.50
All other lettering3.5 mm0.35

No contour lines are required on paved areas whether brick paving, bitumen or concrete. Paving and garden walls in landscaped areas must be measured in and be shown on the drawing. Spot levels are required on corner of buildings, curbs and edges of paving and if necessary also at centre lines of wide driveways and where noteworthy slopes occur at appropriate intervals of more than 100 mm height difference. The FFL (ground floor) of all buildings must be shown.
