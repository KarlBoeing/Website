Assessment 1.2

For this assignment you need to research the various leveling devices and write an assignment covering the performance criteria mentioned below. From Week 1 to Week 3 you do not need to attend the lessons. Research and assignment writing

 

This assignment is applicable for

 

  • BCGBC5006A Apply site surveys and set out procedures

to medium-rise building projects and

  • SIN 27755 Site Surveying & Set Out 2

 

The assignment task:

Part 1

Carry out the essential research for the following surveying instruments:

  • Dumpy Level
  • Automatic Level

·         Laser Level

·         Theodolite

·         Total Station

Briefly list the advantages and disadvantages of all the above researched leveling instruments.

 

Part 2

      Keep in mind the following performance criteria:

·         The various components of the leveling devices must be identified.

·         Any tests required according to manufactures specification?

·         Identify the effects of maladjustment to standard operating procedures.

 

In a short paragraph cover the above performance criteria or learning outcomes for a dumpy level and a theodolite, which need to be researched in greater detail.

 

The assignment must be submitted in Week 4 at the beginning of the lesson.

 

Failure to do so will result in a R-grade

 

 

Every assignment must have attached an Assignment Attachment Form and a Cover Sheet.

Assignments to be handed in as loose paper collection stapled together at the top left corner.

Include photos, diagrams sketches, tables etc., and do not forget to refer to them in your text

Before you write your final assignment, you may discuss your draft with your class lecturer to clear up any problems you may have encountered. Make sure your final assignment is ready by the due date.

It’s expected that you’re familiar with report writing. However, if you have difficulties with report writing, contact the Learning Resource Centre for resources on report writing.
