Please note:

There is no final theory examination. The unit is assessed by practical team work and assignments.

The subject matter in the lessons is not easy to grasp and students should not miss a lesson. Great emphasis is placed on teamwork and students ability to plan activities and solving problems.

Candidates will need to demonstrate competence a number of times under various conditions to satisfy the Element/Performance Criteria

Under CBT and AQTF provisions candidates do not receive a percentage mark for the unit completed.

Candidates demonstrating competence will receive a grade of “CO” (competent). Candidates failing to demonstrate competence at the end of an individual assessment task will receive the grade of “H” (Hold)
and will be required to re-do an exercise/assignment to the required competence level.

Candidates failing to demonstrate competence at the end of the unit will receive the grade of “R” (re-enrol), and will be required to re-enrol in the unit again and complete ALL assessment tasks again to the required competence level.
